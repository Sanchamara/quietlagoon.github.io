Welcome to the Central American Republic of Sanchamara.

It is the eve of 1983 and you are at the helm of power in the capitol building of Calansa de Sanchamara. Despite all odds you and your cadre have seized all facets of government. Will you see your nation through darkness?

Quiet Lagoon is a Cold-War era political simulation which takes place in a fictional Central American republic where various insurgency groups and international superpowers vie for political influence through any means necessary.

This game is still in development
